# Automatic Sand Sieving Machine ⚙️

**Course Project (TA212) | Mentor: Prof. Mohit Law**  
*January 2026 – April 2026*

## 🎯 Objective
To design, automate, and fabricate an effective sand sieving machine capable of separating local soil into distinct grades.

## 🛠️ Approach
* **Mechanical Design:** Modeled a **Scotch Yoke mechanism** in **SolidWorks**, performing kinematic simulations to optimize the sieving frequency.
* **Automation:** Programmed an **Arduino-based** automation system, successfully integrating motor drivers to accurately supervise the sieving cycles.
* **Fabrication:** Manufactured robust structural components from raw steel stock, utilizing drilling and welding techniques to ensure the machine could withstand cyclic loads.

## 🚀 Impact
* Successfully achieved reliable soil separation into **3 distinct grades** by assembling and testing the **final prototype** of the automated soil separator.

## 💻 Tools & Technologies
* **CAD & Simulation:** SolidWorks (Kinematic Simulation)
* **Electronics & Control:** Arduino, Motor Drivers
* **Manufacturing Processes:** Drilling, Welding, Steel Fabrication
* **Key Mechanism:** Scotch Yoke
